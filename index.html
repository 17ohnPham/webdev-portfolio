Trimet Data Pipeline

A TriMet data pipeline collects real-time bus info (GPS location, arrival predictions, service alerts), processes it through a streaming system, and stores it in a central database. The pipeline cleans and normalizes the incoming data, enriches it with route and schedule information, and then publishes usable outputs for analytics. This project was done with a group of 3 other people as part of a Data Engineering class.

Cracked

Cracked is a video game prototype built in Godot, designed by Naton Cai and John Pham. Cracked is a Smash style PVP game developed on the theme of smashing vases similar to video games such as Legend of Zelda. Each player controls a vase, who's goal is to grab their opponent and throw them against the walls and floors. When a vase breaks, they spew out a number of gems that can be picked up.
